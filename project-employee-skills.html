Project Overview

As one of my foundational Power BI projects, I developed a comprehensive solution to address a critical HR and leadership challenge: lack of visibility into team skillsets. Without centralized data, leadership couldn't identify skill gaps, plan effective training programs, or make informed hiring decisions.

This dashboard transformed scattered, anecdotal knowledge into actionable data, providing both high-level executive views and granular individual proficiency tracking across the entire organization.

Solution Architecture

1 Data Collection via Microsoft Forms

Built a structured Microsoft Form for employees to self-assess proficiency levels across multiple skill categories. Form responses automatically feed into Excel for seamless data refresh.

Skill Categories

  • • Programming Languages (Python, JavaScript, etc.)
  • • BI Tools (Power BI, Tableau, etc.)
  • • Cloud Platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • • Frameworks & Libraries

Proficiency Levels

  • • Beginner (Learning fundamentals)
  • • Intermediate (Can work independently)
  • • Advanced (Can mentor others)
  • • Expert (Subject matter expert)

2 Real-Time Power BI Integration

Connected the Excel dataset directly to Power BI with scheduled refresh, ensuring leadership always sees up-to-date skill matrices. Data transformations in Power Query normalize responses and calculate aggregated metrics.

  • Automated data refresh eliminates manual updates
  • Power Query handles data cleansing and transformation
  • DAX measures calculate skill distribution percentages

3 Multi-Page Navigation Dashboard

Designed a 15+ page Power BI report with each page dedicated to a specific tool or technology category. Navigation buttons enable quick jumps between skill areas for focused analysis.

Design Philosophy: Balance detail with usability—executive overview for leadership, drill-down pages for managers, individual views for employees.

Key Features

📊 Department-Level Views

Compare skill distributions across departments to identify areas of strength and teams needing support. Heat maps visualize proficiency clusters.

👤 Individual Proficiency Tracking

Drill down to individual employee skill profiles. Managers can view their team members' expertise and identify development opportunities.

🎯 Skill Gap Analysis

Identify skills with low coverage across the organization. Highlight critical gaps that could impact project delivery or client commitments.

📈 Proficiency Distribution

Visual breakdown of skill levels (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert) helps plan mentorship programs and knowledge transfer initiatives.

🔍 Advanced Filters

Dynamic filters for department, position, skill category, and proficiency level enable custom analysis for specific workforce planning scenarios.

📱 User-Friendly Interface

Intuitive design ensures both technical and non-technical stakeholders can easily navigate and extract insights without training.

Business Impact

Data-Driven Hiring & Training Decisions

Leadership gained visibility into organizational capabilities, enabling strategic decisions on whether to hire externally for new skills or upskill existing employees through targeted training programs.

Optimized Resource Allocation

Project managers could now assign tasks based on actual skill proficiency rather than assumptions, improving project success rates and employee satisfaction.

Strategic Workforce Development

HR could design targeted learning paths and mentorship programs based on skill distribution data, maximizing ROI on professional development initiatives.

Increased Team Transparency

Employees gained clarity on organizational skill inventory, fostering collaboration ("Who can I ask for help with AWS?") and motivating skill development to fill identified gaps.
